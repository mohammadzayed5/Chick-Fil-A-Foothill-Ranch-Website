Leader Development Roadmap

Your path to leadership at Chick-fil-A Foothill Ranch. This roadmap outlines the skills, certifications, and experience needed to advance your career.

1

Team Member Excellence

0-6 Months

Master the fundamentals of your role and demonstrate consistent excellence.

  • Complete all position certifications
  • Maintain excellent attendance and punctuality
  • Demonstrate Second Mile Service consistently
  • Receive positive peer and guest feedback
2

Trainer Certification

6-12 Months

Develop your ability to teach and mentor others.

  • Complete Trainer Certification program
  • Successfully train 3+ new team members
  • Demonstrate strong communication skills
  • Show initiative in helping others succeed
3

Team Leader

12-18 Months

Take on leadership responsibilities during your shifts.

  • Lead shift meetings and huddles
  • Manage small teams during peak hours
  • Complete Team Leader training modules
  • Demonstrate problem-solving abilities
  • Build strong relationships with team members
4

Shift Leader / Director

18-24 Months

Oversee entire shifts and manage operations.

  • Complete Shift Leader certification
  • Manage cash handling and deposits
  • Handle guest recovery situations
  • Conduct team member coaching and feedback
  • Assist with scheduling and labor management
5

Assistant Director / Director

24+ Months

Manage specific areas of the business.

  • Specialize in FOH or BOH operations
  • Manage hiring and onboarding
  • Drive sales and operational goals
  • Develop and mentor Team Leaders
  • Participate in strategic planning
